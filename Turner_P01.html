Cannoli Dip Recipe

This recipe takes less than 10 minutes to prepare, and has lots of room for personalization!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 (8-ounce) package of cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup miniature semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Beat ricotta cheese and cream cheese together in a bowl until smooth; add sugar and vanilla.
  2. Continue to stir mixture until sugar is completely incorporated.
  3. Fold chocolate chips through the cheese mixture.
  4. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, at least 10 minutes.

Prep Time: 10 minutes; Ready in: 20 minutes

Serve chilled with anything you want, examples could be: pizzelles, cookies, or graham crackers
